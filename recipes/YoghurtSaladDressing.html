Yoghurt Salad Dressing

  • 200 g plain yoghurt
  • 3 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt or to taste
  • 1 Tbsp Papper to taste
  • 1 Tbsp Herb mix or to taste
  1. Press garlic
  2. Combine all ingredients and add seasonings
