Vegan Bacon

Ingredients

  • 100g dried tomatoes
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp corn starch
  • 1 tsp coconut sugar
  • 1 tsp smoked salt

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoes into strips or cubes
  2. Combine all other ingredients and mix well
  3. Marinate tomatoes in the sauce for at least 20min
  4. Fry tomatoes from all sides in the marinade
