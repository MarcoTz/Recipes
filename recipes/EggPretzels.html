Egg Pretzels

Ingredients

  • 6 pretzles
  • 6 eggs
  • 300g grated cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C upper/lower
  2. Crack eggs into pretzel holes
  3. Top pretzels with cheese
  4. Bake for 10-15min
