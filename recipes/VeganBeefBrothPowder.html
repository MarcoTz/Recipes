Vegan Beef Broth Powder

Ingredients

  • 4 dried shiitake
  • 0.5 tsp basil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Grind bay leaf and shiitake
  2. Combine all ingredients
