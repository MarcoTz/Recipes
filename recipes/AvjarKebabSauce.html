Avjar Kebab Sauce

  • 75 g creme fraiche or smetana
  • 25 g ketchup
  • 75 g avjar
  • 0.5 tsp cayenne pepper, or more to taste
  • 0.5 tsp garlic powder
  • 0.5 tsp paprika powder
  • 0.5 tsp cumin
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  1. Combine all ingredients and mix well

Notes

1 glass of avjar is usually enough for 1 portions.
