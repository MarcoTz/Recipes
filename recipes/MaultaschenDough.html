Maultaschen Dough

Ingredients

  • 400g flour
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 125ml water
  • 1 Tbsp vinegar
  • 5 Tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients and knead into smooth dough
  2. Rest for 20min in a fridge

Notes

Use with Pork-Spinach Maultaschen or Spinach-Potato Maultaschen

To make maultaschen, follow these steps:

  1. Roll out maultaschen dough to about 3mm
  2. Spread filling on dough and fold over one side of the dough to about 5-10cm
  3. Keep folding over the same side until the entire dough is one long sausage
  4. Use a spoon or similar utensil and press down on the sausage every 10cm
  5. Cut trough the pressed down parts and press dough together
  6. Cook maultaschen for about 10min in salted water or broth
