Sour Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 50g chives
  • 0.5 lemons
  • 400g Quark (1)
  • 200g Creme fraiche
  • 1 Tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Dice garlic and onion
  2. Combine all ingredients and mix well

Notes

  1. Any thick yoghurt/cream works as well
