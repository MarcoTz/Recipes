Sour Cream

  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 50 g chives
  • 0.5 lemons
  • 400 g Quark (1)
  • 200 g Creme fraiche
  • 1 Tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt or to taste
  • 1 Tbsp Pepper to taste
  1. Dice garlic and onion
  2. Combine all ingredients and mix well

Notes

(1) Any thick yoghurt/cream works as well
