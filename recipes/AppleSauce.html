Apple Sauce

Ingredients

  • 6 apples
  • 80ml water
  • 1 Tbsp cinnamon powser
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 0.5 Tbsp vanilla sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash apples, remove cores and dice
  2. Combine all ingredients and cook on low heat for 4 hours
  3. Mash into sauce
