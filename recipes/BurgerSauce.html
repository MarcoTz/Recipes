Burger Sauce

  • 6 Tbsp ketchup
  • 3 Tbsp mayonaise
  • 1 Tbsp creme fraiche
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 0.5 tsp sugar
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp pepper
  • 0.5 tsp paprika powder
  1. Combine all ingredients and mix well
