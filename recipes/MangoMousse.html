Mango Mousse

Ingredients

  • 3 mangos
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 3 Tbsp sugar or honey

Steps

  1. Peel and cube mangos, then puree
  2. Combine with coconut milk and honey
  3. Let cool down in the fridge
