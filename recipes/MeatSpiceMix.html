Meat Spice Mix

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp paprika powder (1)
  • 2 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 Tbsp onion powder
  • 1 Tbsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp coriander
  • 1 Tbsp oregano
  • 0.5 Tbsp thyme
  • 1 Tbsp cumin
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder or more to taste
  • 0.5 Tbsp cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients and mix well

Notes

Works well for vegan meat alternatives to add extra spice

  1. either use spicy paprika or mix spicy with smoked Some sweet paprika can additionally be added
