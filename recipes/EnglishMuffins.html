English Muffins

Ingredients

  • 400ml milk
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 500-600g flour
  • 2 Tbsp dry yeast
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 3 Tbsp cornstarch

Steps

1.Warm up milk (not cooking), mix in sugar and yeast 2. Let rest for 5-10min 3. Combine all ingredients except starch and knewd into a smooth dough 4. Let rest for 1h 5. Dust with cornstarch and roll out to 2-5cm in width 6. Cut the dough into circles about 15cm in diameter 7. Let rest for 20min 8. Fry in a pan at low heat, without any oil, for about 6-7min per side
