Kyabetsu Salad

  • 300 g cabbage
  • 3 Tbsp Mayonaise
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 10 g sesame seeds (optional)
  1. Cut cabbage into small stripes
  2. Conbine all other ingredients (except sesame seeds) to a sauce
  3. Add all ingredients into a big bowl and mix well
