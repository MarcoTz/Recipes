Pizza buns

Ingredients

  • 200g ham
  • 200g salami
  • 200g grated cheese
  • 250ml cream
  • 2 Tbsp oregano
  • 2 Tbsp basil
  • 1 Tbsp paprika powder
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 onion (optional) (1)
  • 5 buns

Steps

  1. Dice ham, salami and onion, mix with cream, cheese and add spices
  2. Let rest for 2 hours
  3. Preheat oven to 200C upper/lower
  4. Half the buns and spread the topping on them
  5. Bake for 20-25min

Notes

  1. alternatively, use onion powder
