Yoghurt Salad Dressing

Ingredients

  • 200g plain yoghurt
  • 3 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Papper to taste
  • Herb mix to taste

Steps

  1. Press garlic
  2. Combine all ingredients and add seasonings
