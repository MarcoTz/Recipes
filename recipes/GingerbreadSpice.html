Gingerbread Spice

Ingredients

  • 30g cinnamon
  • 10g cloves
  • 6g ginger
  • 4g kardamom
  • 4g nutmeg
  • 4g pimento
  • 2g koriander
  • 2g anise

Steps

  1. Use a pestle and mortar to grind all spices
  2. Combine all spices
