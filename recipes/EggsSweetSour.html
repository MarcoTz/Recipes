Eggs Sweet-Sour

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 150g rice
  • 80g brown sugar
  • 150ml pineapple juice (1)
  • 80ml rice vinegar
  • 60ml soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp ketchup
  • 2 tsp corn starch

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Fry eggs, over-hard
  3. Mix all other ingredients and pour over eggs
  4. Simmer until the sauce thickens
  5. Serve over rice

Notes

  1. can be replaced by water
