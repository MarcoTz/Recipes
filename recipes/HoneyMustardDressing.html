Honey Mustard Dressing

  • 4 Tbsp mustard
  • 6 Tbsp yoghurt
  • 2 Tbsp honey
  • 6 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp salt
  1. Mix all ingredients

Notes

For a vegan version use agave sirup instead of honey
