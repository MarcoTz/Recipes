Broccoli Pesto

  • 1 broccoli stem
  • 50 g pine seeds
  • 80 ml olive oil
  • 50 g grated parmesan
  • 1 tsp salt or to taste
  1. roast pine seeds in a pan
  2. Combine all ingretients and pulse in food processor until smooth
