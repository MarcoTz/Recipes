Broccoli Pesto

Ingredients

  • 1 broccoli stem
  • 50g pine seeds
  • 80ml olive oil
  • 50g grated parmesan
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. roast pine seeds in a pan
  2. Combine all ingretients and pulse in food processor until smooth
