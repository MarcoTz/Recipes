Mango Lassi

  • 200 g fresh mango
  • 250 g yoghurt
  • 80 ml milk
  • 3 Tbsp honey or sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom (optional)
  1. Combine yoghurt, milk and mango in a blender and blend until smooth
  2. Top with caramom and serve
