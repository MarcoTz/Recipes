Sriracha

  • 500 g chili peppers (any kind or mixed)
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 250 ml water
  • 150 ml rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  1. Chop chilis and garlic
  2. Combine all ingredients in a pot, bring to a boil
  3. Simmer for 10-15min, remove from heat
  4. Add to a food processor and blend until smooth
