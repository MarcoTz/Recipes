Big Mac Sauce

Ingredients

  • 60g mayonaise
  • 1 Tbsp dill relish (or pickle water)
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup (or honey or agave syrup)
  • 1 Tbsp onion powder
  • 1 Tbsp apple vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients and mix well
