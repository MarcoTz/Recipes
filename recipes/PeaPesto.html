Pea Pesto

  • 250 g peas
  • 0.5 lemons
  • 40 ml olive oil
  • 2 spring onions
  • 0.5 Tbsp pepper or to taste
  • 0.5 Tbsp chili flakes or to taste
  • 80 g Feta cheese (optional)
  1. Add all ingredients to a mixer and mix until smooth
