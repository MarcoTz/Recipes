Pea Pesto

Ingredients

  • 250g peas
  • 0.5 lemons
  • 40ml olive oil
  • 2 spring onions
  • pepper to taste
  • chili flakes to taste
  • 80g Feta cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a mixer and mix until smooth
