Pesto Bowl

Ingredients

  • 4-5 potatoes
  • 200g mini mozzarella balls
  • 3-4 Tbsp green pesto
  • 20 cherry tomatoes
  • 50g pine seeds

Steps

  1. Cook and peel potatoes, dice once they have cooled down
  2. Roast pine seeds in a pan
  3. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well
