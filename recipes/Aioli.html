Aioli

  • 130 ml Mayonaise
  • 2 cloves garlic or more to taste
  • 0.5 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt or to taste
  1. Combine all ingredients with a hand mixer
  2. Rest in fridge for at least 30min
