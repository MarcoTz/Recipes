Yoghurt-Pesto Schupfnudeln

Ingredients

  • 500g Schupnudeln
  • 200g Tomatoes
  • 200g orange pesto
  • 0.5 cucumber
  • 150g plain yoghurt

Steps

  1. Pan-fry Schupfnudeln
  2. Dice tomatoes and cucumber
  3. Mix all ingredients together
