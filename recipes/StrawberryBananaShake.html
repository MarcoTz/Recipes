Strawberry-Banana Shake

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 100g strawberries
  • 2 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 Tbsp yoghurt
  • 150ml mlik
  • 2 Tbsp cocoa powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a mixer and mix until smooth
