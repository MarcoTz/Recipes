# Chickpea Cookie Dough

## Ingredients

* 380g chickpeas (cooked)
* 60g butter
* 1 tsp vanilla sugar
* 3 Tbsp rolled oats
* 2 Tbsp honey or maple syrup
* 60g chocolate chips

## Steps

1. Combine all ingredients except chocolate chips in a mixer and mix until smooth
2. Add chocolate chips
