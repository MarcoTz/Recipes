# Wurstsalat

## Ingredients

- 250g Lyoner
- 200g cheese
- 5_ pickles
- 2_ onions

### Sauce

- 1 Tbsp mustard
- 5 Tbsp neutral oil
- 1 Tbsp pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 50ml broth
- 3 Tbsp vinegar
- 1 tsp paprika

## Steps

1. Combine all sauce ingredients and cut all others into small cubes/slices.
2. Cover with sauce and leave in the fridge over night

## Notes

Optionally add Schwarzwurst, Bell Peppers or eggs
