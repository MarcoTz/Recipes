# Goulash

## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp neutral oil
- 2_ onions
- 500g cubed beef
- 4_ bell peppers
- 5_ cloves garlic
- 3_ potatoes
- 300g diced tomatoes
- 1l broth
- 2 Tbsp tomato paste
- 2 Tbsp sweet paprika powder
- 2 Tbsp spicy paprika powder
- 1 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 Tbsp pepper
- 1_ bay leaf
- 2_ carrots (optional)
- 1 tsp caraway (optional)

## Steps

1. Dice onions, potatoes, peppers and carrots, mince garlic
2. Pan-fry onions in oil until transparent
3. Add garlic and continue another 30s
4. Add meat and tomato paste, fry until done
5. Add potatoes, peppers and carrots, continue until slightly browned.
6. Add tomatoes and broth, bring to a boil
7. Reduce heat, add spices then simmer for at least 1 hour (1)

## Notes

(1) the longer, the better
