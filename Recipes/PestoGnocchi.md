# Pesto Gnocchi

## Ingredients

* 400g [Gnocchi](Gnocchi.html)
* 250g dried tomatoes
* 250g green pesto
* 300ml heavy cream
* 1 tsp salt or to taste

## Steps

1. Cook gnocchi according to package instructions
2. Dice tomatoes and pan-fry from all sides 
3. Stir in cream and pesto, season with salt 
4. Stir in gnocchi
