# Korokke

## Ingredients

- 800ml frying oil

### Filling

- 900g potatoes
- 1_ onion
- 1 Tbsp neutral oil
- 500g ground beef or mixed vegetables
- 1 Tbsp salt
- 0.5 Tbsp pepper
- 1 Tbsp butter

### Breading

- 60g flour
- 120g panko
- 3_ eggs

## Steps

1. Wash, peel and cut quarter potatoes, then cook them until soft
2. Mince and pan-fry onion, then add meat and spices
3. Once potatoes are done, remove water and keep cooking on very low heat, until
   most water is evaporated.
4. Mash potatoes then mix with the remaining filling
5. Form mixture into balls of about 5cm diameter
6. Let them cool down completely, then rest in refrigerator for 10-15min
7. Roll each korokke in flour, then egg, then panko
8. Fry in oil until orange-brown
