# Yoghurt Salad Dressing

## Ingredients

* 200g plain yoghurt 
* 3 Tbsp lemon juice
* 1 Tbsp olive oil
* 1 tsp mustard 
* 1 clove garlic
* 1 tsp sugar 
* 1 tsp Salt or to taste
* 1 Tbsp Papper to taste
* 1 Tbsp Herb mix or to taste

## Steps 

1. Press garlic
2. Combine all ingredients and add seasonings
