# Vegan Oyakodon

## Ingredients

- 1_ block deep fried tofu
- 3_ eggs
- 0.5_ onion
- 1_ spring onion
- 150g rice

### Broth

- 200ml dashi
- 1.5 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1.5 Tbsp mirin
- 1 tsp sugar

## Steps

1. Mix dashi, soy sauce, mirin and sugar in a pan.
2. Slice onion into half circles and add to broth
3. Separate white and green parts of spring onion, slice all into long strips
4. Add white parts of the spring onion to the broth
5. Cut tofu into cubes, add to broth and soak for 1-2min
6. Turn over tofu, then turn on the heat and simmer broth for 3min
7. Beat eggs and slowly pour egg over the broth
8. Top with remaining spring onion
9. Cover and cook for about 1min until egg whites are set
10. Serve over rice, optionally with shichimi
