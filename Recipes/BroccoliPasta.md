# Broccoli Pasta

## Ingredients

- 1_ head broccoli
- 300g pasta (any)
- 250ml cream
- 3_ cloves garlic
- 1 Tsp white wine
- 1_ onion
- 250ml vegetable broth
- 50g parmesan
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp pepper

## Steps

1. Mince garlic, chop onion and cook broccoli and pasta
2. Fry onions until transparent, add garlic and continue about 30s
3. Add wine to garlic and onions, cook until mostly evaporated
4. Add broth, cream and broccoli, bring to a boil, then reduce heat
5. Simmer for 5-10min then add spices and parmesan
