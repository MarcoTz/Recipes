# Vegan Chicken Broth Powder

## Ingredients

* 60g yeast flakes
* 1 tsp sugar
* 1 tsp garlic powder
* 1 Tbsp salt 
* 1 Tbsp onion powder
* 0.5 Tbsp tumeric
* 1 tsp thyme
* 1 tsp oregano
* 1 tsp basil
* 1 tsp celery salt (1)
* 1 tsp sage

## Steps

1. Combine all ingredients

## Notes

(1) Altenatively. use 0.5 tsp of dried celery and increase salt by 0.5 tsp
