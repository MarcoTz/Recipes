# Cowboy Pasta

## Ingredients

- 300g Pasta
- 400g Minced Meat
- 1_ onion
- 2_ bell peppers
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 250g kidney beans
- 150g grated cheese
- 2 Tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp sweet paprika
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 0.5 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp frying oil

## Steps

1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
2. Mince onion and peppers
3. Fry meat in oil, then add onion, peppers and tomato paste and continue frying
4. Add tomatoes and beans, bring to a boil
5. Reduce heat and add spices
6. Stir in pasta and simmer for about 5 min
7. Add cheese and turn off heat
