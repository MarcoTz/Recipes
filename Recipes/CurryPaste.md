# Curry Paste

## Ingredients

- 50g mixed chillies
- 0.5tsp white pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 1_ stalk lemon grass
- 2 Tbsp galangal (1)
- 4_ cilantro roots (2)
- 2 tsp lime zest
- 6_ cloves garlic
- 70g chopped shallots
- 2 tsp shrimp paste or miso paste

## Steps

1. Chop chilles, garlic and shallots
2. Combine all ingredients in a pestel and mortar and combine to a smooth paste

## Notes

(1) ginger is also possible if galangal is not available (2) 2 tsp cilantro
powder also works
