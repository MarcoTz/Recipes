# Feuerzangenbowle

## Ingredients

- 2l red wine
- 300-500ml dark rum
- 200g sugar
- 2_ oranges
- 5_ cloves
- 2_ cinnamon sticks
- 4_ star anise

## Steps

1. Heat up wine, make sure it does not cook
2. Slice oranges, and add to wine with cloves, anise and cinnamon
3. Steep for at least 1h, the longer the better
4. Before serving, stir in rum and sugar
