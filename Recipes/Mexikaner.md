# Mexikaner

## Ingredients

* 0.7l Korn
* 1l tomato juice
* 500ml pureed tomatoes
* 0,7l Sangrita picante (one bottle)
* 80ml lemon juice
* 40ml tobasco
* 80g tomato paste
* 4 Tbsp sugar
* 3 Tbsp salt
* 3 Tbsp pepper, or more to taste
* chili powder to taste (optional)

## Steps

1. Combine all ingredients and mix well
2. Leave in the fridge over night
