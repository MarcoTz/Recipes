# Marzipan

## Ingredients
 
* 90g ground almonds 
* 70g powdered sugar 
* 1.5 Tbsp water 
* 0.5 tsp almond extract 
* 0.5 tsp rose water (optional) 

## Steps

1. Combine almonds and powdered sugar in a mixer and mix until smooth
2. Knead in water and rose water
