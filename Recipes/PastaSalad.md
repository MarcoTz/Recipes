# Pasta Salad

## Ingredients

- 500g Rotini Pasta
- 3_ eggs
- 300g peas and carrots
- 3_ pickles
- 200g corn
- 200g cheese (edam, gouda or similar), (optional)
- 3_ sausages (optional)

### Dressing

- 250g mayonaisse
- 1 tsp mustard
- 1 Tbsp broth powder
- 1 Tbsp pickle juice
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 Tbsp Pepper
- 1 Tbsp paprika powder
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp sugar (optional)

## Steps

1. Cook pasta according to package instructions, and hard-boil eggs
2. Dice vegetables, Cheese and cheese, combine dressing ingredients
3. Mix everything together
