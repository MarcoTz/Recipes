# Vegan Kebab

## Ingredients

- 150g soy chunks
- 1l vegetable broth
- 2 Tbsp tomato paste
- 3 Tbsp soy sauce
- 3 Tbsp vegetable oil or olive oil
- 4 Tbsp [Kebab Spice](KebabSpice.md)
- 2 Tbsp vegetable broth powder
- 2_ bay leaves (optional)

## Steps

1. Cook soy chunks in vegetable broth and bay leaves
2. let fully cool down and press out remaining water
3. Mix remaining ingredients to a marinade and massage into the soy chunks
4. Let rest for at least 15min, better at night
5. Panfry in vegetable oil until crisp
