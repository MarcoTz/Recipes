# Dampfnudeln

## Ingredients

- 300ml milk
- 50g butter
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 1 Tbsp sugar

### Dough

- 250ml milk
- 100g butter
- 500g flour
- 60g sugar
- 1 Tbsp salt
- 10g yeast
- 2_ egg yellows

## Steps

1. Combine dough ingredients and knead into smooth dough.
2. Rest for 45min or until volume doubles
3. Separate dough into 5 parts and form into balls. Rest for another 30min
4. Combine remaining ingredients in a pot and bring to a simmer.
5. Add dough balls and steam for 30min with closed lid.
6. Rest for another 5min with the lid on and heat off before serving

## Notes

Usually served with vanilla sauce

vegetarian, dessert
