# Creamed Savoy

## Ingredients

- 1_ head savoy
- 1_ onion
- 100g ham / sausage
- 250ml broth
- 75ml milk
- 1 Tbsp flour
- 1 tsp butter
- 100ml cream
- 1 tsp salt
- 0.5 Tbsp pepper
- 1 tsp nutmeg

## Steps

1. Dice onions, savoy and ham, melt butter in a pan
2. Add onions and ham to butter, pan-fry until slightly brown
3. Add savoy and broth, simmer on low heat.
4. Combine flour and milk and ad to pan, bring to a boil
5. Reduce heat and add spices
6. Stir in cream and serve
