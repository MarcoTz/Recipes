# Potato Gratin

## Ingredients

- 1kg potatoes
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 120g Parmesan
- 300ml cream (1)
- 2 Tbsp Flour
- 3 Tbsp butter
- 200g grated cheese (2)

## Steps

1. Melt butter in a pot, add flour to create a roux.
2. Add in butter an cream
3. Slowly bring to a simmer and cook until the sauce thickens.
4. Add parmesan (and optionally additional cheese)
5. Simmer until the cheese is fully incorporated
6. Preheat oven to 200C upper-lower
7. Cut potatoes into thin slices
8. Layer sauce and potatoes in a buttered baking pan (3)
9. Top with remaining cheese
10. Bake for 45-55 min

## Notes

(1) alternatively, use half cream, half milk.

(2) you can also add more cheese and add some into the sauce with the Parmesan

(3) here, you can also add additional cheese between the layers
