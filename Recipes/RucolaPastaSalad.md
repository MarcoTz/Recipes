# Rucola Pasta Salad

## Ingredients

- 300g Rotini Pasta
- 200g Rucola
- 250g cherry tomatoes
- 3 tsp Capers (or olives)
- 50g parmesan sliced

### Dressing

- 50ml olive oil
- 1_ lemon juiced
- 2_ cloves garlic
- 2 tsp chopped basil
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp salt

## Steps

1. Cook pasta according to package instructions
2. Combine pasta with rucola, capers, tomatoes and parmesan
3. Mix dressing ingredients and mix with salad
