# Flatbread

## Ingredients

- 200ml water
- 1 tsp olive oil + 1 Tbsp for wash
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 350g flour
- 50g yoghurt
- 15g instant yeast
- 1 tsp sesame seeds or black caraway

## Steps

1. Combine dry ingredients
2. Stir in wet ingredients and knead into a smooth dough
3. Let rest for at least 45min or until the volume has doubled
4. Knead dough and separate into 4 balls
5. Let rest for another 15min
6. Preheat oven to 220 upper-lower
7. Cover bread with olive oil and sprinkle sesame on it
8. Bake for 15-20min
