# Tzatziki

## Ingredients
 
* 1 cucumber 
* 250g greek yoghurt
* 1 Tbsp lemon juice 
* 0.5 Tbsp olive oil
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 Tbsp dill
* 1 Tbsp mint (optional)

## Steps

1. Finely grate cucumber and garlic
2. Mix all ingredients together and let rest (best overnight)
